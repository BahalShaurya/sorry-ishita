🇮🇳  India  ·  Nepal  🇳🇵

A letter I owe you,
written with my whole heart

Miles apart, but none of that distance matters as much as this — making sure you know how I feel, and how deeply I mean every single word.

💛

What I put you through

Every argument that was my fault — you carried the weight of it. We were both at home, in our own worlds, an entire country apart, and instead of being your peace across that distance, I added to your stress. You never deserved that. Not once.

I imagine how exhausting it must have been — to be patient with me, to still reach out, to hope I'd understand — and I didn't always rise to that. I am genuinely, deeply sorry for every moment I made you feel unseen, hurt, or unvalued.

🌸

What I should have been

You needed someone steady. Someone who made the distance feel smaller, not heavier. You needed a partner, not another source of worry. And there were moments I failed that — where I chose being right over being kind, where I let my insecurities fill the space between us.

You gave me so much grace, across so many miles. I didn't always honour that the way I should have.

🌷

What I promise you, for real this time

  • 🤍
    I will pause before I speak — no more saying things in the heat of the moment that I don't mean.
  • 🌷
    I will listen first and argue second — because your feelings matter more than me winning a fight.
  • ✨
    I will not lean on "sorry" as a patch. I will show you change through action, not words.
  • 📱
    I will check in on you more — ask how you're feeling, be present and attentive, not just available.
  • 🌸
    I will be the safe space you should have always had in me — understood, never judged.
  • 🖤
    I will hold myself accountable without needing you to call me out every time.
🕊️

What I want you to know

I'm not asking you to forget any of it. I'm not asking you to pretend it didn't hurt. All I'm asking is for the chance to be better — to show up as the person you deserve, every single day.

The distance between India and Nepal is nothing compared to how close I want us to feel. You mean more to me than I've probably made you feel lately. That changes now.

With everything I have — always. 🌸
